# Focus Management

### 1. What is Deep Work?
* Deep work means, working on particular task without getting distracted. Giving our full focus on that work and in the end which leads to best results.

### 2. According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
* Schedule the distractions at home and at work.
* Developing a rhythmic routine. Like we dont need to decide what to do, we have to make deep work as routine.
* Early mornign is the best time to go in deep work.
* Evening shutdown. Means let go of tasks early from mind and have good sleep. 

### 3. How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
* I will try to finish the work early and let go of tasks from my mind which leads to good sleep.
* I will wake up early in the morning which has the best time to do deep work.

### 4. What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
* It distracts us and reduces our focus.  
* It wastes a lot of time without us realizing it.  
* It can increase stress and anxiety.  
* It affects deep thinking and productivity.
