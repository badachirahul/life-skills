* In this talk, Angela Duckworth explains that success is not mainly about talent but about “grit,” which means having passion and perseverance for long-term goals. She emphasizes that people who keep working hard and stay committed despite failures are more likely to achieve success.

### Question 2: Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines in your own words.
* The video explains that a growth mindset is the belief that our abilities and intelligence can improve through effort, practice, and learning. It shows that when we see challenges and failures as opportunities to grow, we become more motivated and resilient.
