# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
* Bad comments on a co-worker’s attire.
* Using abusive words in conversation.
* Giving more work because the employee is a woman.
* Asking for an outing again and again even though they are not interested.

## 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
* We have to keep a record of what happened, the dates of the incidents, and the location.
* Even after asking the harasser to stop the abusive behavior, if he continues, then report a complaint to the ICC.
